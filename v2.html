About

Shiver Wallet is a web-based light wallet that permits offline signing of transactions. This allows you to keep your seed safe on an air-gapped computer, and only access it when you need to sign something. Your seed will never touch a computer that's connected to the Internet, dropping possible attack vectors to nearly zero.

Simply download a copy of this webpage onto a flashdrive (You should only need one file, download.html. It's completely self contained). Use a device connected to the internet to gather the data requried for a state/send block. Transfer it to your air-gapped computer and sign the transaction using your private key (theres a seed --> private key tool at the bottom of the page), then send it on the Internet-connected device.

Guide

I've tried to make the process of sending and receiving Nano as painless as possible and the instructions clear.

  1. Get a Nanode API key. You'll need it to gather the data from the network and send your signed block.
  2. Download this page. It's entirely self-contained, so all you need is your download.html file. Transfer it to a recently-formatted flash drive and for maximum security do it on a new operating system/virtual machine.
